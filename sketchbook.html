Welcome to my sketchbook!


Growing up, I had always taken weekly art classes, but I didn't start pursuing art more seriously as a hobby until about two years ago, when I attended California State Summer School for the Arts (CSSSA) in 2016. Since then, I've been trying to improve at portraiture, and the more I practice, the more I recognize there's still a lot I have to learn! I'm also interested in experimenting more with lighting and environment concept art.
